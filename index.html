Waterford Based Alternative Indie Rock

Formed in Waterford in 2020, Deaf Monkey play a brand

of high energy punchy guitar driven indie rock/pop.

Deaf Monkey

Drawing inspiration from multiple genres from shoegaze to psychedelic rock and rock to post punk with little pieces of everything else inbetween, Deaf Monkey have crafted a sound that's definetly their own.

Unfiltered, emotional and raw.
